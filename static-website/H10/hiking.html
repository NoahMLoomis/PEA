Hiking Trips

We offer a number of fun and varied hiking trips, from day-trips to week-long expeditions, whatever adventure you're looking for your feet to take you, you'll find it here!

4 Day Hiking Trip-Yosemite

See the world-famous Yosemite National Park located in California! Our 4 day trip will take you to all the major peaks, including the famous "El-Capitan", a unique flat piece of granite almost 3000 meters high!

Day Trips-Adirondacks

Hike the world famous moutain range,start your journey on becoming a "46er", a challenge to summit all 46 peaks over 4000 feet! Trips are very adjustable to whatever peaks you would like to do.
